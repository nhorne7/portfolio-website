In the Summer of 2025, I travelled to the Arctic University of Norway, Tromsø (UiT) to conduct novel research in Nanophotonics. I further designed a novel dynamics-based nanoparticle morphotype classification pipeline, which is the basis of a forthcoming first-author publication in the Journal of Nanoparticle Research.

Following my bachelor’s degree, I intend to enroll in the Elite Master Program of Theoretical and Mathematical Physics, a joint initiative of Ludwig-Maximilians Universität München (LMU) and Technische Universität München (TUM), aimed at training leading physicists in the field.
